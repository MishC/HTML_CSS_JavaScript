Honey belongs to the so-called superfoods, of all natural products, has the highest and balanced content of basic sugars and a wide range of compound sugars that are essential for a living organism and also contains countless vitamins, minerals, enzymes and medicinal substances, with antibacterial, antioxidant, immunostimulatory and revitalizing effects.

Our home made honey is made from nectar from Robinia (false acacia). The tree is known as an ornamental tree in many places, but grows wild in Serbia, among other places. The honey is called acacia honey internationally, although the name is a bit misleading in terms of origin. The acacia honey stays liquid for a long time, and has a mild and sweet taste. It is extremely suitable for most things, and has therefore become a favorite honey for many. See more about how we breed bees.
