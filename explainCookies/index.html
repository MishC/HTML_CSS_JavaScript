Cookies

What are cookies?

Cookies are files created by websites that you visit. By saving information about your visit, they make your online experience easier. For example, sites can keep you signed in, remember your site preferences and give you locally relevant content.

Types of cookies

  • First-party cookies

    • Created by the site that you visit. The site is shown in the address bar.

  • Third-party cookies

    • Created by other sites. A site that you visit can embed content from other sites, for example images, ads and text. Any of these other sites can save cookies and other data to personalise your experience.

Security

It's important for websites to handle cookies responsibly and transparently, ensuring that users are informed about the types of data being collected and have the option to opt out if desired. These rules are enforced by GDPR (The General Data Protection Regulation).
