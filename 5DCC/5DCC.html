Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a very caloric Italian food. Indeed carbonaro is the Italian word for charcoal burner, some believe the dish was first made as a hearty meal for Italian charcoal workers.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • 2 eggs and one yolk
  • Cheese Grana Padano or Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Pancetta (type of bacon)
  • 1 clove of garlic.
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 20g of butter

Preparation

  1. Cook Spaghetti in boiled water with salt until al dente.
  2. Mix eggs with grated cheese, salt and pepper and put aside.
  3. Warm the pan for a minute and add olive oil and little of butter.
  4. Add cutted pancetta to the pan and cook until it starts to crisp up.
  5. Peel the garlic, then crush with the palm of your hand, add it to the pan and leave it to flavour the fat and pancetta for 1 minute. Then take it out from the pan.
